ACME AG Travel & Expense Policy (v2025-01)
- Hotel: Max CHF 250 per night in Switzerland; CHF 200 elsewhere in Europe.
- Flights: Economy for trips under 6 hours; Premium Economy if >6 hours with manager approval.
- Per diem: CHF 75/day domestic; CHF 90/day international; alcohol not reimbursable.
- Taxis vs Public Transport: Use public transport when practical; taxi allowed after 22:00 or if carrying heavy equipment.
- Approvals: Manager approval required for any exceptions; Finance audits claims > CHF 1,500.
- Receipts: Required for any expense >= CHF 25.
